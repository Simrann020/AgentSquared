## About SneakerCo

**Our Story**  
SneakerCo was founded in 2019 in Denver, Colorado by two lifelong sneaker collectors who were frustrated with the experience of buying authentic sneakers online: too many fake products, too little transparency on stock, and customer service that never answered questions. We set out to build the sneaker store we always wanted to shop at — 100% authentic, fully transparent on inventory, and obsessively focused on the customer.

**What We Carry**  
We are an authorized retailer for Nike, Jordan Brand, Adidas, New Balance, Vans, and Converse. Our catalog focuses on the most iconic and sought-after silhouettes from each brand — the shoes that have proven themselves over years and decades as true classics. We do not chase every hype drop; we stock what will still matter in ten years.

**Our Team**  
We are a team of 12 sneaker enthusiasts based between Denver, Colorado and a warehouse in Louisville, Kentucky. Every person on our team was hired first for their passion for sneakers. We believe if you love the product you will take better care of the customer.

**Our Commitment**  
Every sneaker ships from our Louisville warehouse within 24 hours. Every product is inspected before shipping. Every customer gets a response within 4 hours on business days. These are not aspirations — they are standards we hold ourselves to every single day.

**The Community**  
SneakerCo is more than a store. Our community forum has over 8,000 members discussing releases, styling fits, and helping each other find the right shoe. We show up in that forum every day to answer questions, share knowledge, and be part of the conversation — not just the transaction.

## Shipping Policy (Last updated: January 1, 2026)

**Processing Time**  
All orders are processed within 24 hours of placement Monday through Friday, excluding public holidays. Orders placed on weekends are processed the following Monday. You will receive an email confirmation with tracking information once your order ships.

**Domestic Shipping Options**  
- **Standard Shipping (5–7 business days):** Free on orders over $100. $7.99 on orders under $100.  
- **Express Shipping (2–3 business days):** $14.99 flat rate regardless of order size.  
- **Overnight Shipping (next business day):** $34.99 flat rate. Orders must be placed before 12pm EST to qualify. Weekend delivery not available for overnight shipping.

**International Shipping**  
SneakerCo ships to over 50 countries worldwide.  
- **International Standard:** 10–14 business days — $24.99  
- **International Express:** 5–7 business days — $44.99  
Duties and taxes are the responsibility of the recipient. Import fees vary by country and are not included in our shipping fees. Please check your local customs regulations before ordering.  
Countries we ship to include: Canada, UK, Australia, France, Germany, Japan, South Korea, Netherlands, Sweden, Spain, Italy, Brazil, Mexico, and 37 more. Contact us if your country is not listed.

**Order Tracking**  
Once your order ships you will receive an email with your tracking number. Track at sneakerco.com/track or directly on the carrier website. Standard orders use USPS or UPS depending on location. Express and overnight use UPS or FedEx.

**Shipping Restrictions**  
We cannot ship to PO Boxes for express or overnight. Some products may have shipping restrictions due to brand authorization zones; this is rare and will be communicated at checkout if applicable.

## Returns & Exchanges (Last updated: January 1, 2026)

**Return Window**  
SneakerCo accepts returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, in original condition, and in the original box with all accessories and tags attached. We cannot accept returns on worn items or items without original packaging.

**How to Start a Return**  
Email returns@sneakerco.com with your order number and reason for return. We will send a prepaid return label within 24 hours. Pack the item securely in the original box, attach the label, and drop it at any UPS location. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days of receiving the returned item.

**Refunds**  
Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Credit card refunds appear within 3–5 business days after processing. PayPal refunds appear within 24 hours. Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a defective item.

**Exchanges**  
We offer free exchanges within 30 days. Email exchanges@sneakerco.com with your order number and what you would like instead. We will ship the new item once we receive the return. If the new item costs more you will be charged the difference; if it costs less we will refund the difference.

**Defective Items**  
If you receive a defective or incorrect item, contact us within 7 days at support@sneakerco.com with photos. We will send a replacement immediately at no cost and arrange return pickup of the defective item.

**Sale Items**  
Items purchased during flash sales (50% off or more) are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged. All other sale items follow the standard return policy.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Orders and Payments**  
- **Track order:** Once your order ships you receive an email with a tracking number. Track at sneakerco.com/track or on the UPS/USPS website. Tracking updates every 24 hours. No tracking email within 2 business days? Contact support@sneakerco.com.  
- **Payment methods:** Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay. All transactions are encrypted and secure. We do not store your full card details.  
- **Cancel order:** Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that the order enters processing and cannot be cancelled. Contact support@sneakerco.com immediately if you need to cancel before it ships.  
- **Gift cards:** Yes. Digital gift cards in $25, $50, $100, and $200. Delivered by email within minutes, never expire. Purchase at sneakerco.com/gift-cards.  
- **Price matching:** We do not offer price matching with other retailers. We run our own promotions; sign up for our newsletter to be notified.

**Products and Stock**  
- **Authenticity:** Every sneaker we sell is 100% authentic, sourced from brand-authorized distributors or the brands themselves. We do not sell replicas. Every order includes an authenticity guarantee.  
- **Sizing (US):** Measure your foot and compare to the brand’s official size chart. Nike and Jordan tend to run half a size large. New Balance runs true to size. Adidas runs small — size up half a size. Vans and Converse run large — size down half a size.  
- **Out of stock:** Means we have sold through current inventory. Most items are restocked; we show expected restock dates on product pages when we have confirmed info. Sign up for restock notifications on the product page.  
- **Limited editions/collabs:** Yes, occasionally. Sign up for our newsletter or follow @sneakerco on Instagram. Limited releases often sell out quickly.

**Discounts and Loyalty**  
- **Student discount:** 10% off for students with a valid .edu email. Email students@sneakerco.com from your student email; we send a personal code within 24 hours. Code valid 12 months, once per month.  
- **Loyalty program:** 1 point per dollar spent. Points accumulate automatically with the same email. 100 points = $5 off your next order. Points never expire. Check balance and redeem at sneakerco.com/account.  
- **Current promo codes:**  
  - SPRING20 — 20% off all New Balance styles, expires March 31, 2026  
  - FREESHIP — Free shipping on any order, expires April 7, 2026  
  Sign up for newsletter for future codes.

**Support and Community**  
- **Community forum:** Yes — join thousands of sneaker enthusiasts. Our support team and AI assistant Aria monitor the forum daily to answer questions.  
- **Customer support:** support@sneakerco.com. Response within 4 hours Monday–Friday, within 24 hours on weekends. For urgent order issues, mention URGENT in the subject line.
